PASSED OR FAILED

GCAT will announce student scores for the OLSAT and SPM Examination. After that, a formal announcement will be made if the student passed their preferred course or not.

GORDON COLLEGE ADMISSION AND TESTING

The Gordon College Admission and Testing Unit office is open for student questions regarding GCAT examination results. The GCAT Portal will announce if you have passed or not.

WHAT TO DO IN THIS PHASE

  • Monitor the GCAT portal for your results.
  • If you passed, there will be a button to accept or decline your slot.
  • If not passed, you will proceed to your second course choice.
  • You have 5 days to decide if you will continue in this program.
  • After confirming, you will receive a notification for passing documents.

KEY NOTES FOR RESULTS

  • OLSAT — 72 questions (you need to score Average or Above)
  • SPM — 60 questions (you must score 50 points to secure your slot)
  • Wait at least 3 days for results — many students are evaluated simultaneously.
  • You have only 5 days to decide if you will accept your course.
CONTACT FOR GCAT: info.gcat@gordoncollege.edu.ph

ENROLLMENT PROCESS

The final administrative step to officially register as a student. This involves the submission of verified credentials and the generation of your official student load or schedule.

GORDON COLLEGE CCS FACULTY

CCS will be in charge in the student enlistment and enrollment process. You will go to CCS to confirm your enrollment and to BSIT you will show your unit to be enlisted and enrolled.

Activation of your student portal (Gordon College Enrollment System) and your Learning Management System (GCLAMP). These are essential for viewing grades and accessing virtual classrooms.

WHAT IS GCLAMP & GCES

  • GCES is the administrative database for grades and registration.
  • GCLAMP is the Google-integrated platform used for coursework, quizzes, and class materials.

WHAT TO DO

  • Log in immediately to update your profile and verify that your registered subjects are correctly listed.
